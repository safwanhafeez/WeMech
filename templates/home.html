The Porsche 356, produced from 1948 to 1965, rear-wheel-drive layout. It was offered with various air-cooled flat-four engines, ranging from 1.1 to 2.0 liters, paired with 4-speed manual transmissions (later models had 5-speed options). Available in coupe, convertible, and Speedster body styles, the 356 was known for its lightweight construction, timeless design, and agile handling, making it a classic among sports car enthusiasts.
